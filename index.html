About Me

My name is Hasan Abbas, a Syrian photographer. I hold a BA in Information Technology Engineering from Tartous University. Photography was a great passion for me and it is my favorite hobby.

In 2015 I bought my first professional camera and with time I worked on developing myself in various fields of photography.
In 2017, I opened my first studio and expanded my work more and more, so I entered the field of videography and worked on developing skills in photos and videos and editing each of them.
I trained in several courses in the academic principles of photography and photo editing programs.
And with the beginning of 2022, I moved to live and work in Erbil, Iraq. I work in advertising photography for social media pages, in addition to filming weddings and events as a freelancer.

I hope you like my gallery, waiting for your opinions.
